Common Questions

Do I need an appointment?+

Yes. Every build is scheduled so we can give your vehicle the time it needs. Call or send the form and we will find you a slot.

How long will my vehicle be with you?+

It depends on the service. Tint is often same day, while full body film and coating work takes longer. We will give you a clear timeline with your quote.

Do you work on brand new vehicles?+

Yes, and that is the best time to come see us. Protecting a new vehicle from day one is far easier than fixing damage later.

Can I get more than one service at once?+

Absolutely, and most customers do. Film, coating, and tint together is the package we recommend for any new vehicle.
