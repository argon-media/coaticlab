Paint
Protection Film

An invisible shield against rock chips, scratches, and UV.

Paint Protection Film is a durable urethane layer that acts as a barrier between your paint and the road. It self-heals light marks with heat and keeps your finish looking new.

Pricing

Paint Protection Film: $1,099 to $1,599+, depending on coverage and vehicle.

Coverage Tiers

Four options

01

Drivers / Partial Front

Front bumper, front half of hood, fog lights, partial fender, side mirrors, headlamps.

02

Full Front

Front bumper, full hood, full fender, headlamps, fog lights, door cups, side mirrors.

03

Full Body Gloss

Full-vehicle protection with a natural gloss that lets the paint shine through.

SunTek Coverage Slot
04

Full Body Matte

Full-vehicle protection with a matte finish for a distinct, eye-catching look.

Gloss Finish

Keeps your factory shine and depth. The film is virtually invisible, protecting the paint while letting its true color and gloss show through.

Matte Finish

Converts a glossy finish to a smooth satin look while delivering the same protection. A distinct, understated appearance that stands apart.

Windshield Protection Film

Protect your windshield from chips and cracks with Windshield Skin film.

Ask About It

How long does PPF last?

With proper care, quality paint protection film lasts for years. We will go over expected lifespan and care for your specific film during your consultation.

Will the film yellow over time?

Modern urethane films are designed to resist yellowing and stay clear. Regular maintenance keeps them looking their best.

Does the film self-heal?

Light swirls and marks fade with heat from the sun or warm water, thanks to the film's self-healing top layer.

Can PPF be removed later?

Yes. Professionally installed film can be removed without damaging factory paint.

How long does installation take?

It depends on coverage and vehicle. We will give you a clear timeline with your quote.

Ceramic
Coatings

Lasting gloss, easy maintenance, and protection through every season.

A ceramic coating forms a chemical bond with your paint, boosting shine and adding a hydrophobic layer that makes washing and drying effortless.

Coating Levels

01

Level 1

Chemical resistance, strong durability, and an ultra-smooth, hydrophobic finish. Washing and drying is a breeze.

02

Level 2

Maximum chemical and UV resistance with added slickness that keeps contaminants from sticking, plus a deep glossy shine and easy maintenance.

Wheel

Glass

Trim

Interior

Coating Maintenance

We inspect your coating, fully decontaminate the paint, apply a fresh top coat, and include a full exterior detail. Recommended yearly.

Pricing

Ceramic Paint Coating: $1,299 to $1,699+
Coating Maintenance: $399 to $1,299+

As a Gyeon-certified dealer, we install coatings to the manufacturer's standard, with the training and process to back every layer.

How long does a ceramic coating last?

Durability depends on the product level and how the vehicle is maintained. We will recommend the right level and a maintenance schedule.

Does a coating replace washing?

No. It makes washing far easier and helps your paint stay cleaner, but regular washing is still part of care.

Is a coating the same as wax?

No. A ceramic coating forms a chemical bond with the paint for far more durability and gloss than wax.

Do I need paint correction first?

A coating locks in the finish underneath it, so we correct defects before coating for the best result.

Paint Correction

Our meticulous paint correction removes swirls, scratches, and defects using machine polishers, compounds, and specialized pads to restore the clearest result possible. Final results depend on your vehicle's paint condition.

Pricing

Paint Correction: $500 to $1,000+, by vehicle size and paint condition.

Detailing Packages

From $249 to $499+

01

Exterior Detail

A thorough exterior wash, decontamination, and finish to bring back shine.

02

Interior Detail

Deep cleaning of surfaces, carpets, and upholstery for a fresh cabin.

03

Interior Ceramic + Detail

A full interior detail plus a ceramic treatment that protects surfaces and makes cleanup easier.

04

Interior & Exterior Package

Our complete detail, inside and out, for a fully refreshed vehicle.

What does paint correction remove?

Machine polishing removes swirls, light scratches, oxidation, and other surface defects to restore clarity.

Will it fix every scratch?

Deep scratches that reach below the clear coat may not fully correct. Final results depend on your paint's condition.

Should I pair correction with a coating?

Yes. Correcting first and then coating locks in the refreshed finish and protects it.

How long does it take?

It varies with vehicle size and paint condition. We will give you a timeline with your quote.
