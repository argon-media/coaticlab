Family Is the Foundation

At CoaticLab Automotive Studio, family is not just part of the story. It is the foundation of everything we do.

We are a proud, family-owned business that has served Northern Utah for six years. Blake, the majority owner, and his wife Katie work on site daily overseeing quality control. Blake's parents, Sean and Lana, support the mission behind the scenes as minority owners. Together with a dedicated team of five skilled professionals, we treat every customer's vehicle with the highest standard of care.

That tight-knit passion has fueled real growth, taking CoaticLab from a humble single-unit shop to a brand-new, state-of-the-art facility that has already doubled in size. Choosing CoaticLab means supporting a local family business built on hard work and an unwavering commitment to excellence.
