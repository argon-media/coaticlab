What Is Window Tint?

Window tint is a thin film applied to your vehicle's glass that controls how much heat, light, and UV get through. Done right, you keep the visibility and clarity you already have, and lose the heat, glare, and interior fading that come with it.

Not all tint is equal. Traditional dyed and metallic films block some light but let heat through, or interfere with your signals. Ceramic tint uses ceramic particle technology to reject far more of the sun's infrared heat while staying completely signal-safe. It is the film we install and the one we recommend.

Window Tint FAQ

Is your tint legal in Utah?+

Yes. Every install is done to Utah's legal limits for each window, so your vehicle looks right and stays compliant.

Will ceramic tint really keep my car cooler?+

Yes. Ceramic tint rejects far more infrared heat than dyed or metallic film, which is what you feel as a cooler cabin and less AC use.

Will tint mess with my phone or radio signal?+

No. Our ceramic film is non-metallic, so it does not interfere with Bluetooth, cellular, or radio.

How dark can I go?+

As dark as Utah law allows for each window. We will show you the legal options and help you pick a look.

How long does the install take?+

Most tint jobs are completed the same day. We will give you a clear timeline with your quote.

How long before I can roll my windows down?+

The film needs time to cure. We will tell you exactly how long to wait when you pick up your vehicle.

Can I add tint to a coating or PPF package?+

Absolutely, and most customers do. Film, coating, and tint together is the full trifecta.
